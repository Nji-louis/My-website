About Us

Gold Trim Salon is a Modern Unisex beauty salon dedicated to delivering high-quality grooming and styling Services
in a clean, stylish, and welcoming environment. We specialize in a wide range of services including haircuts, braiding,
wig installation, hair coloring, beard grooming, nail care, training and more.
Our mission is help you look and feel your best by combining professionale expertise with the latest trends in beauty
and hair care

Located in Logbessou, Douala, we specialize in haircuts for men, women & kids, braiding, wig installations, nail &
pedicure Services, beard grooming, and more.

We are also an academy, training the next generation of beauty professionals.
